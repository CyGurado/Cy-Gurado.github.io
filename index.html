About Us

Cygurado aims to establish cybersecurity awareness among Liceo students. Our aim is to protect students against online threats with educational materials, security guidelines, and available means of reporting cyber incidents.

What do we do?


  • Educate the students on best practices in cybersecurity
  • Keep them updated with the information on cyber threats
  • Provide the means to report suspicious online activity
  • Allow students to take an active role in maintaining a secure digital environment


Join us in making the digital world a safer place!
