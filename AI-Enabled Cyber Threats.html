AI-Enabled Cyber Threats

    AI is being used to enhance various cyber threats:

  • Chatbot abuse: Cybercriminals are manipulating chatbot prompts for malicious purposes, such as generating malicious scripts or bypassing programmed restrictions.
  • Social engineering campaigns: AI is used to create sophisticated phishing pages, generate text for business email compromise (BEC) schemes, and create scripts for call service operators.
  • Deepfakes: AI is used to create fake audio and video to impersonate individuals and businesses, enabling disinformation campaigns and scams.
  • KYC verification bypasses: AI is used to create realistic-looking photos of identification cards and bypass facial verification systems.

    Other threats and concepts:

  • Exploitation of vulnerabilities: Public-facing applications is a common method of initial access..
  • Compromised credentials: Valid accounts are also used for unauthorized access.
  • Commodity loaders: These tools, bought on the dark web, like Qakbot, are used to gain access to systems.
  • Advanced Persistent Threats (APTs): Long-term, stealthy attacks often by state-sponsored groups.
  • Initial access vectors: These are the methods that attackers use to gain entry into a system such as exploiting vulnerabilities, using compromised credentials, or phishing.
  • Living Off The Land (LOTL): Cyberattacks where criminals use legitimate IT administration tools to carry out malicious activities.
  • Business Email Compromise (BEC): A type of social engineering attack where attackers impersonate business personnel to gain access to sensitive information or funds.
  • Online Disinformation: The spread of false information through online channels.
  • Supply Chain Attacks: Attacks that target third-party vendors or software providers to infiltrate larger organizations.
  • Code Injection Attacks: Techniques such as SQL Injection and Cross-Site Scripting (XSS), used to execute malicious scripts in poorly secured web applications.
  • Brute Force Attacks: Systematically guessing login credentials to gain access.
